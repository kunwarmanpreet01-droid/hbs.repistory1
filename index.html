Building a Brighter Tomorrow

Founded in 2043 B.S. (1986 A.D.), Himanchal Boarding School is a 21st century value-based progressive school located at Airport-7, Pokhara, Nepal. We believe in nurturing not just academic brilliance but also character, creativity, and compassion.

Our commitment to quality education combines rigorous academics with enriching extracurricular programs. We believe in learning by doing, with emphasis on the practical aspect of education.

  • Co-educational English Medium Institution
  • State-of-the-art Science & Computer Labs
  • Comprehensive Sports & Cultural Programs
  • Digital Learning Environment with Smart Classrooms
  • Safe Transport & CCTV Surveillance
